About

I'm Janet Delavan, a self-taught illustrator and communication design undergrad at Parsons School of Design in NYC. With a passion for learning new things, and discovering new interests, my style is constantly evolving. My illustrations focus on joyful compositions and warm color palettes to make life a little brighter, as graphic design goes, my style may contrast my illustrations quite a bit. I believe in approaching every project with mindfulness and determination to make each creation better than the last. When I'm not designing, I spend my free time playing tennis, getting late-night coffee, and spending time with her friends!
